Chicken Quesadilla

This is a simple recipe for a chicken quesadilla.
  • Chicken breast
  • Tortilla
  • Monterrey Jack cheese
  • Butter
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  1. Grab the chicken breast and salt and pepper to both sides, fire up a skillet and cook the chicken thoroughly
  2. Cut the cooked chicken into cubes or strips.
  3. Grab a tortilla and spread some butter on the tortilla.
  4. Put a heaping amount of cheese on one half of the tortilla followed by the chicken. Fold the tortilla over.
  5. Place the quesadilla on the skillet and cook the tortilla until golden brown and the cheese is melted.
  6. Remove the quesadilla from the skillet and cut the quesadilla into four equal pieces and enjoy.
